Korean Eyes on E-skin (AR)

Worklog 1 Mar 2024




E-skin is an extension of our skin. It exists through electric circuitry, an extension of our central nervous system, in our phones.

Why e-skin matters. South Korea is known for the fastest Internet and cybersex crime cases. Sexism is deep-rooted in the country but is almost not speakable, socially. AR as e-skin can be a way to make people engage with female and other bodies.

Living in Paris, I have experienced a woman’s desire. There, this desire is quite institutionalised. There are social codes as well as literature and philosophy. It is becoming a social norm.

It is time for me to act in South Korea in connection with the global society. How e-skin can shape us.



Designing E-skin

1) Mimicry and Evolution

Animal hierarchy: Human white male > … > insect/swarm/bug

Spanish Fly (blister beetle family)
 A blister beetle's ovary is filled with Cantharidin.   © Thomas Eisner

I imagine human lips mimicking a blister beetle.

A blister beetle has Cantharidin inside. It is a gift from the male to female through their caress, a nutrition to their spawns. And, it is toxic to their predators. Their colorisation has evolved to warn.

A woman or others, whose e-lips would evolve through mimicry of a blister beetle, or of "love," would protect themselves while attacking some people visually. Expressing this meta-sexuality through gestures is discussed below in 3).

2) Racial Color Matters

As e-skin is beyond the geographical borders, its colors are composed with racial colors. The color of e-skin generates different beauty of skin colors while letting them non-hierarchic. This may be affected by my Asian background. Invading the scheme of black and white, yellow creates another dynamics. And, e-skin makes physical body colors more prominent. Skin is new beauty.

3) Choreography for an AR Camera

Performing with e-skin develops the mimicry explained in 1). This expresses the meta-sexuality as if voguing expresses queer sexuality. Choreography and technical test are in progress.


Test on Technical Limitation

Date: 240213
Subject: Lips movements
Software: Meta Spark AR (v.178)
Result:

Face Recognition
- Face on a horizontal camera view is not recognized. Full face is needed.

Motion Perception
- 3D graphics have lower performance than real images. With the graphics, in general, a smaller face on screen and faster motion result in better appearance.
- Global movement, such as turning a face and approaching to the camera, is subject to subtle discrepancy between e-skin and skin.
- Local or micro-movement, such as smiling, is less subject to the discrepancy.




After this trial, thoughts are left.

Can e-skin be an innovation? Would it blur the conventional animacy hierarchy, or more generally allow us to experience wildness in the society?

Can e-skin be FemTech? Most services, driven by the notion of FemTech, are medical in the current market. Enhancing our skin (not only facial) differently could reveal a female or other body to us.
