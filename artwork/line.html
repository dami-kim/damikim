Line (2018-9)

© Collin Kluchman

Performance
Durational, masking tape 8m, printed A4



Description: A performer enters a room. They read the artist's instruction.

Line is a performance space.
Face me,
Walk towards me upon the line.
When you step out of the line, the performance will be finished.
On the line, the artist imitates the performer’s movement. They never stage this meeting before. It happens in a closed space without audience.
