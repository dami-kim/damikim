Line, 2018-9


© Collin Kluchman

Description A performer enters a room. They read the artist's instruction. On the line, the artist imitates the performer’s movement. They never stage this meeting before. It happens in a closed space without audience.

Instruction
Line is a performance space.
Face me,
Walk towards me upon the line.
When you step out of the line, the performance will be finished.

Performance, durational, masking tape 8m, printed A4
