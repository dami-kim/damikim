Desire and Deception (2019)

Performance Video
4’54

The artist pushed the audience into intimacy while under the surveillance of others – not only the other audience members but also someone beyond the camera. As a result, they were whether to keep desiring as a social self or to be nervous about their own deception. The artist’s touching movements emphasized this conflict manifested upon their bodies.

This video piece is an extraction of In front of the mirror (2018).
