에로스, 발톱을 드러내어  
Eros, as a Modern Punishment
2021 Solo Show

Performance re-enactment with audiences in South Korea.
This show was organized by the artist and produced independently from the state-run art institutions in order not to be censored in any way.
Due to COVID-19, small audiences could watch the performances on site, and the performance videos were live-streamed on the web.

ACT I    27 Mar, Geumnarae Art Hall

ACT II   17 Sep, Bosu-dong Street

ACT III  13 Nov, Horanggasy Art Polygon

Web   www.moderneros.live
